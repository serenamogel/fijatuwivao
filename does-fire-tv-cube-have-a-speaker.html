Does Fire TV Cube have a speaker?

May 29, 2024
blog
Like other streaming devices, the Fire TV Cube does have a built-in speaker and microphone that you can use to play audio or control the device via Alexa. However, it's usually best to stream audio through your TV or a compatible soundbar or surround sound system to get the best quality.

Does fire cube have audio output?

The Fire TV Cube does not have an audio output. It uses an HDMI audio pass through up to 5.1 and a built-in speaker. Despite the lack of audio output on the Fire TV Cube, it still offers plenty of alternatives to traditional sound systems.
