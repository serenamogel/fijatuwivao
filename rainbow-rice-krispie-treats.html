How To Make Rainbow Rice Krispie Treats

  • Prep. Line a 9 inch or 8 inch square baking dish with parchment paper and lightly spray with nonstick cooking spray.
  • Marshmallow Mixture. Melt the butter in a large, heavy bottomed dutch oven or pot over medium-low heat, stirring frequently. Add the marshmallows and salt. Stir continuously until the marshmallows are completely melted and smooth.
  • Add Cereal. Remove the pot from heat and gently fold in the cereal until combined with the marshmallow mixture. Transfer to the prepared baking dish and gently press into an even layer using a spatula or hands.
  • Set Up. Allow the rice krispie treats to cool completely. Remove the bars from the baking dish and cut into squares. Enjoy!

    • Storage Information

    These rice krispie treats are best stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days.

    Can this recipe be doubled or tripled?

    These adorable rice krispie treats are such a crowd pleaser you may want to double or triple the recipe to feed a crowd. Just be sure to use a larger pot and mixing bowl to accommodate the extra ingredients.

    Trish’s Tips and Tricks

    With just a few simple ingredients, you can create a colorful and delicious rice krispie treat that everyone will love! Here are some tips so you get the best results:

    • If you don’t have a 9 inch square baking dish, you can use a 9×13 inch instead. The treats will be thinner. Likewise, you can use an 8 inch pan, and the treats will be thicker.
    • Make sure to stir the marshmallows constantly while melting them to prevent them from burning.
    • Add a few drops of food color to the marshmallow mixture for even more color.
    • Gently fold in the cereal to avoid crushing it.
    • Lightly spray or grease your knife before cutting the treats. You can also rub some butter on the knife.
    • Try different types of cereal in this recipe, such as cocoa pebbles, to create new flavor combinations.
    • You can add all sorts of fun mix-ins, like chopped candy bars, dried fruit, or even sprinkles to this recipe – the possibilities are endless!

    Rainbow Rice Krispie Treats

    These delicious and colorful Rainbow Rice Krispie Treats are an easy, no-bake treat that's delightfully crunchy, wonderfully sweet and perfectly gooey! Easy to make and even easier to eat, these rice krispie treats get a fruity burst of flavor and a pop of color from Fruity Pebbles – a real crowd pleaser!

    Course Dessert Cuisine American Keyword Easter Rice Krispie Treats, rainbow rice krispie treats Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Cook Time 5 minutes minutes Servings 16 Calories 148kcal Author Trish – Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
    • 12 ounces miniature marshmallows
    • ½ teaspoon salt
    • 4 ½ cups rice krispies cereal
    • 2 ½ cups fruity pebbles cereal

    Instructions

    • Line a 9 inch or 8 inch square baking dish with parchment paper and lightly spray with nonstick cooking spray.

    • Melt the butter in a large, heavy bottomed dutch oven or pot over medium-low heat, stirring frequently.

    • Add the marshmallows and salt and stir continuously until the marshmallows are completely melted and smooth, about 2 to 3 minutes.

    • Remove the pot from heat and gently fold in the rice krispies and fruity pebbles until combined with the marshmallow mixture.

    • Transfer to the prepared baking dish and gently press into an even layer using a spatula or hands.

    • Allow the treats to cool completely. Remove the bars from the baking dish and cut into squares. Enjoy!

    Notes

    Storage Information These rice krispie treats are best stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days.
