Guy Hands (born 27 August 1959) is an English banker and investor who works in the financial services industry. For his work as the founder and chairman of Terra Firma Capital Partners, one of the biggest private equity companies in Europe, he is perhaps best known. Hands has also served as Chairman of EMI, a music business based in the United Kingdom. Accordin to the 2011 Sunday Times Rich List, Hands and his wife were reported to be worth £100 million, putting them at number 703 on this list of the wealthiest individuals in the United Kingdom. Hands is well-known for his frequent and vehement criticism of the private equity sector, which he has expressed publicly. Terra Firma’s headquarters are in the tax haven of Guernsey, where he relocated from the United Kingdom in April 2009.
