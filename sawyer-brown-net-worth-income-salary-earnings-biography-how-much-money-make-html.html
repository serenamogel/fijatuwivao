Sawyer Brown is a country music band from Apopka, Florida, formed in 1981 by five members of country pop singer Don King’s road band: Bobby Randall (guitar) and Jim Scholten (bass guitar), both from Midland, Michigan; Joe Smyth (drums), Gregg “Hobie” Hubbard (keyboards), and Mark Miller (bass guitar) (lead vocals). Following King’s retirement in 1981, the five members decided to start a band, initially naming it Savanna before changing it to Sawyer Brown, which is also the name of a road near where they practised.
