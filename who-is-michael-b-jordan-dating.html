Who is Michael B. Jordan dating?

February 17, 2024
blog

With his career-defining roles as Adonis Creed in all the Creed movies as well as Killmonger in Black Panther, Michael B. Jordan is considered one of the most promising and talented actors today. His directorial debut in Creed III received critical acclaim, solidifying his fame and popularity as well as strengthening a swelling fan base.

An increasing popularity of this kind comes with a rising interest in personal life and, not unlike his celebrity cohorts, Jordan is no exception to this rule. Recently, the actor’s dating life has sparked a lot of curiosity among his fans who are keen to know about his current romantic interest.

Even though he successfully kept his dating life under wraps until now, word is out that the Creed star is romantically involved with the British model, Amber Jepson. Saying so, a glimpse into Jordan’s dating history would depict a vast array of women that he had dated, some of which are Hollywood A-listers.

Michael B. Jordan and Amber Jepson’s relationship, explained

Jordan and Jepson started dating quite some time now. As per The Sun, they met and started to spend time with each other after the 36-year-old actor purchased a stake in football club AFC Bournemouth.

A source closer to the couple told The Sun, “It’s early stages but Amber and Michael are really keen on each other. They make a handsome, cute couple and are both financially independent. Watch this space.” The couple has quite often been seen together since they started dating and according to the source, Jordan’s head has been turned by the model.

While the actor’s romantic life is a popular subject of discussion, fans have also shown interest to know more about Amber Jepson. The latter is a 26-year-old Manchester-based model who’s part of the well-known modeling agency, Boss Model Management.

A social media influencer with over 300,000 followers on Instagram, Jepson has worked for a couple of clothing and beauty brands. As far as the conversation about Jordan’s relationship goes, both he and Jepson seems to be pretty close at the moment.

However, this also invites the topic of Jordan’s past love interests. The Creed III star has an impressive dating profile, where various renowned actresses, singers, and models have made the list.

Probably his most serious relationship up till now was with model Lori Harvey, daughter of television personality Steve Harvey. This was also the first time the Black Panther actor publicly confirmed their relationship. On Dec.1, 2021, Jordan spoke to The Hollywood Reporter on this subject, implying how happy he was and also regarded Harvey as his ‘special person.’ He said:

“When I was younger, I don’t know if I could have handled the onslaught of opinions and being picked apart, and also being conscious of what the other person’s going to go through. It takes a special person to deal with that. There’s a premeditated planning of staying out of the way [of the public eye] that sometimes kills spontaneity and intimacy. I wanted to take that away and give this the best chance it had, in this weird world that we live in, to be somewhat normal. Long story short, I think it’s just the timing of everything. It was the right time for me. Yeah. I’m happy.”

Unfortunately, the relationship couldn’t last long as the couple called it quits in June 2022. A source closer to them said that both Lori and Jordan were completely ‘heartbroken’ after the breakup, but they still were cordial to each other.

The actor’s dating list also includes rapper Iggy Azalea, model Cindy Bruna, Kiki Layne, and Kendall Jenner.

While Jepson and Jordan seem to be in a happy relationship today, fans are always eager to know more about them and, most significantly, how they will work out in the future.
