Who owns the backlog in Scrum?

February 16, 2024

The owner of the Scrum Product Backlog is the Scrum Product Owner. The Scrum Master, the Scrum Team and other Stakeholders contribute it to have a broad and complete To-Do list. Working with a Scrum Product Backlog does not mean that the Scrum Team is not allowed to create and use other artifacts.

Who owns and prioritize the backlog?

In real Scrum, the Product Owner is the one that prioritizes the product backlog. However, it is the Development Team that decides how many of the prioritized stories it can fit in the upcoming Sprint.

How do you manage backlogs in agile?

To keep your product backlog manageable, it's best to follow these simple tips:

  • Review the backlog periodically.
  • Delete items you'll never do.
  • Keep items you are not ready for off the backlog.
  • Do not add tasks unless you plan to do them soon.
  • Always prioritize.
