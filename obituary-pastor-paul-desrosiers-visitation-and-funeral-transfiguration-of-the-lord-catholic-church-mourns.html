It is with deep sorrow that we must inform you of the passing of our Pastor and dear friend, Fr. Paul Desrosiers, V.F. We will miss him greatly.

We are sorry to be the ones to deliver this disappointing news to you. We are forced to tell you the tragic news that he has passed away, and it is with a heavy heart that we do so.

We are forced to break the news to you that this most recent turn of events has taken, but it is with a heavy heart that we do so because we have no other choice.

On Tuesday, the viewing will begin at 9:30 in the morning and continue all the way through the afternoon, coming to a conclusion at 12:00.

On Tuesday, November 29, 2017, at twelve o’clock in the afternoon, this event is scheduled to take place as part of the schedule.
