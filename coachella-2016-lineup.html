Coachella Drops Impressive 2016 Lineup

March 03, 2024
blog

Recommended Videos

Coachella just dropped their 2016 lineup, and it’s absolutely huge. Headliners come in the form of LCD Soundsystem, who we heard would be there not too long ago, Guns N’ Roses and Calvin Harris. Support, meanwhile, is equally as impressive, with the hottest names from all genres of music making up the rest of the list.

EDM superstar Diplo will be performing twice it looks like, as both Jack Ü and Major Lazer are set to play, while fellow dance music A-listers Zedd, Disclosure, The Chainsmokers and more are all going to be making appearances. The more underground side of EDM isn’t forgotten about either, as Nicole Moudaber, Adam Beyer, Maceo Plex and several other exciting talents will also be playing.

While the lineup is certainly heavier on the electronic side than it has been in previous years, there’s still plenty to get excited for here even if EDM isn’t your thing. Whether it be Halsey, Ice Cube, Ellie Goulding, Sia, A$AP Rocky, etc., there truly is a little something for everyone on this year’s roster and we cannot wait to get there in just a little over 4 months.
