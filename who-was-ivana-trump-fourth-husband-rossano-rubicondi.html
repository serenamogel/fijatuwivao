Ivana Marie Trump was the ex-wife of former US President Donald Trump and a Czech-American entrepreneur, media personality, fashion designer, author, and former model. She was born on February 20, 1949.

She spent the 1970s in Canada before moving to the United States, where she married Donald Trump in 1977. She held important management roles in The Trump Organization as vice president of interior design, CEO and president of Trump’s Castle casino resort, and manager of the Plaza Hotel.

Who was Ivana Trump fourth husband Rossano Rubicondi?

Before marrying on April 12, 2008, Trump dated Italian actor and model Rossano Rubicondi for six years. Ivana, at 59, married Rubicondi, 36, for the fourth time.

Ex-husband Donald Trump sponsored the couple’s $3 million wedding for 400 guests at Mar-a-Lago, with daughter Ivanka serving as maid of honor. Donald’s sister, Judge Maryanne Trump Barry, presided at the wedding.

Even though Ivana and Rubicondi divorced less than a year later, their on-again, off-again relationship lasted until 2019, when Ivana declared they had “called it quits” once more. Rubicondi died at the age of 49 on October 29, 2021.
