Who is Pamela Anderson?

Pamela Denise Anderson, born on July 1, 1967, is a Canadian-American actress, model, and media personality renowned for her work in glamour modelling for Playboy magazine and her iconic role as "C.J." Parker on the television series Baywatch (1992–1997).

Her journey to fame began when she was chosen as Playboy magazine's Playmate of the Month for February 1990, leading to numerous appearances on the magazine's covers, setting a record for the most Playboy covers by any individual. Anderson's popularity grew in 1991 with her role as Lisa on the ABC sitcom Home Improvement for its first two seasons. However, it was her portrayal of "C.J." Parker on Baywatch that truly solidified her status as a sex symbol on an international scale.

Throughout her career, Anderson played Vallery Irons on the syndicated series V.I.P. (1998–2002) and starred as Skyler Dayton on the Fox sitcom Stacked (2005–2006). She also has an extensive filmography, including movies like Raw Justice (1994), Barb Wire (1996), Scary Movie 3 (2003), Superhero Movie, Blonde and Blonder (both 2008), The Institute, Baywatch (both 2017), and City Hunter (2018).

She even ventured into the world of reality TV, participating in shows like Dancing with the Stars (2010, 2012), Bailando (2011), VIP Brother (2012), Dancing on Ice (2013), and Danse avec les stars (2018). In 2023, Anderson collaborated with Netflix on Pamela, a Love Story, a documentary showcasing her life.

Beyond her entertainment career, Anderson is a passionate advocate for various charitable causes, particularly animal rights, and actively supports PETA activities. She has authored three autobiographies and four novels, displaying her diverse talents. In 2006, she was honored with a star on the Canadian Walk of Fame, and she holds dual citizenship in both the United States and Canada.
